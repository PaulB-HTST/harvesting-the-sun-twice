BESS option — Battery Energy Storage System

— adds storage CapEx, replacement costs and optional arbitrage revenue
VBPV BESS Paradox: the east-west generation profile reduces required BESS capacity by approximately 52% compared to equivalent TMPV — because generation is spread across the full day rather than peaking at midday. This significantly reduces the whole-system cost of adding storage.
