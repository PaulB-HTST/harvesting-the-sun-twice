CfD Revenue Model — VBPV vs TBPV

Under UK Contracts for Difference, revenue is withheld when wholesale prices go negative. VBPV’s morning and evening generation peaks avoid the midday glut where negative prices occur — capturing more of the strike price and requiring significantly less battery storage.
